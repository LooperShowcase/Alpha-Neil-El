    fun fact about pufferfish!

    The pufferfish, also known as a blowfish, can inflate itself like a balloon to ward off predators. What's even cooler is that their skin and internal organs contain tetrodotoxin, a poison that's 1,200 times more lethal than cyanide. Despite this, in Japan, the pufferfish (called fugu) is considered a delicacy, prepared only by highly trained chefs!
